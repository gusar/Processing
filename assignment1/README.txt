Assignment 1

Little space survival game.

Menu:
	-Start game on medium difficulty
	-Choose difficuly
	-Exit

Game:
	-Survive an alien invasion by evading enemies.
	-Min and max speed of enemies increases every 5 points.
	-Hull strength and fuel are limited.
	-Collect energy tanks to earn:
		-Points
		-Fuel
		-A life every for every 5 points.

Controls:
	-Left and Right arrow keys for controls (alt: 'a' and 'd').
	-P for pause during a game.
	-Q to quit to menu.
